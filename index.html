About Repowering Partners

Repowering Partners is a solar asset acquisition and optimization firm focused on purchasing existing solar facilities that are aging, underperforming, or financially distressed.

As the first generation of U.S. utility-scale solar projects matures, many assets face declining production, outdated equipment, and increasing operational complexity. Our mission is to unlock hidden value in these legacy systems.

We provide detailed asset evaluation, helping owners understand the current performance, identify areas for improvement, and maximize long-term financial returns. This includes analyzing energy output, component health, maintenance history, and regional incentive programs.

Investment Focus

Aging Facilities

Mid-to-late-life projects requiring upgrades or repowering. Our team identifies where improvements can increase efficiency, extend operational life, and optimize ROI. This includes inverter health checks, module performance assessments, and predictive maintenance planning.

Distressed Assets

Financially or contractually challenged solar facilities. We evaluate risk, uncover hidden value, and implement strategic solutions. Assessment covers contract obligations, incentive opportunities, and cost-saving measures to stabilize operations and improve profitability.

Underperformance

Projects producing below modeled expectations. Through detailed diagnostics, we pinpoint technical, operational, or design-related issues. We provide actionable recommendations for efficiency gains, energy yield optimization, and long-term output reliability.

Utility & C&I Scale

Assets with long-term operational potential. Our approach maximizes return while maintaining reliable performance at scale. Evaluations include site energy forecasts, DC-to-AC optimization, and comprehensive O&M strategies for sustainable asset performance.

Industry Insight

Many U.S. solar projects commissioned between 2010 and 2016 were built under early design assumptions and evolving O&M practices, leading to structural underperformance.

Understanding regional incentives, federal programs, and market trends is crucial to accurately valuing solar assets. Repowering Partners combines market intelligence with on-the-ground performance analysis to identify opportunities others may overlook.
