How It Works
Simple.
Transparent.

We've built a streamlined acquisition process for operating solar assets — aging systems, distressed sites, equipment issues, end-of-life facilities. Four steps from submission to payment.

01

Submit

Tell us about your asset — location, size, and a few basic details. Takes under five minutes.

02

Valuation

Our team reviews your asset and delivers a no-obligation valuation within 72 hours of submission.

03

Offer

We make a competitive, all-cash offer. Sell as-is — we handle PPA renegotiations, interconnection agreements, and all the paperwork.

04

Close

We manage the full closing process. You walk away funded with no development work required on your end.

Operating
Solar Assets.

We buy operating solar installations across the U.S. — not development projects or new builds. If your asset is producing power, we want to hear from you.

Asset Types
  • Ground mount solar installations
